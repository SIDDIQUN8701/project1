Any package exceeding 150 pounds or bigger than 75 inches in any dimension is not valid for regular transport and must go through UPS Freight. UPS Freight is a more commercially used branch of UPS Shipping, but still essential nonetheless. If you're in the middle of a move and don't have any room in the truck for the furniture, or you're a company looking to transport your equipment to different stores around the continental United States, UPS Freight is the shipping choice for you. Because of the complicated nature of UPS Freight, we advise you visit the "Freight" page on the UPS Corporate website tab located at the top of your screen for more information.
